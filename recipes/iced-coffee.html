Iced coffee

Description

Iced coffee made with an immersion dripper

Ingredients

  • coffee powder
  • boiling water

Steps

  1. Boil water.
  2. Place the coffee filter in the dripper.
  3. Put the coffee powder in the dripper.
  4. Pour boiling water into the dripper and wait 3 minutes.
  5. Place the dripper on top of the server to brew the coffee.
  6. Add ice to the coffee.
